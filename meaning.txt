Planet Meanings and LabelsEach planet imparts specific energies, with adjectives summarising their vibe:

	•	Sun - Vigorous: Vitality, leadership, confidence; ideal for bold actions, public dealings, or health rituals. High energy, assertive.

	•	Venus - Beneficial: Harmony, love, creativity, wealth; best for relationships, arts, finance, or beauty. Positive, prosperous.

	•	Mercury - Quick (or “QuickCountdown Sandbox” likely a app-specific note): Intellect, communication, commerce; suits learning, trading, writing, or tech tasks. Fast-paced, adaptable.

	•	Moon - Gentle: Emotions, intuition, nurturing; good for family, rest, meditation, or home matters. Calm, receptive.

	•	Saturn - Sluggish: Discipline, endurance, restrictions; for serious work, planning, or solitude. Slow, methodical, challenging.

	•	Jupiter - Fruitful: Growth, wisdom, abundance; favors teaching, legal matters, expansion, or charity. Optimistic, rewarding.

	•	Mars - Aggressive: Action, courage, conflict; best for sports, arguments, repairs, or defense. Dynamic, intense.
